What Is Health?

Health refers to a state of complete emotional, mental, and physical well-being. Healthcare helps people maintain or improve overall health and quality of life.

Physical Health

Physical health means the body’s systems are working at their best through exercise, nutrition, rest, and preventive medical care.

  • Minimizing workplace hazards
  • Practicing proper hygiene
  • Avoiding tobacco, alcohol, and illegal drugs
  • Taking recommended vaccines

Mental Health

Mental health refers to emotional, social, and psychological well-being. Good mental health helps people enjoy life, adapt to stress, and reach their full potential.
